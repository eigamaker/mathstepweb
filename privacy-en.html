Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

eigamaker (the "Company") has established this Privacy Policy (the "Policy") regarding the handling of personal information of users in the mobile application "MathStep" (the "App").

1. Personal Information

"Personal Information" refers to "personal information" as defined in the Personal Information Protection Act, and includes information about living individuals that can identify specific individuals through names, dates of birth, addresses, phone numbers, contact information, and other descriptions contained in the information, as well as data related to appearance, fingerprints, voice prints, and health insurance card numbers that can identify specific individuals from the information alone (personal identification information).

2. Collection Methods of Personal Information

The Company may ask for personal information such as name, date of birth, address, phone number, email address, bank account number, credit card number, driver's license number, etc. when users register for use. We may also collect transaction records and payment information including users' personal information from our partners (including information providers, advertisers, ad distributors, etc., hereinafter "Partners").

3. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The purposes for which the Company collects and uses personal information are as follows:

  1. To provide and operate the Company's services
  2. To respond to inquiries from users (including identity verification)
  3. To send emails about new features, updates, campaigns, etc. of the services users are using and other services provided by the Company
  4. To contact users as necessary for maintenance, important notices, etc.
  5. To identify users who violate the Terms of Service or who use the service for fraudulent or improper purposes and refuse their use
  6. To allow users to view, modify, delete their registered information and view their usage status
  7. To charge users for paid services
  8. Purposes incidental to the above purposes of use

4. Changes to Purpose of Use

1. The Company may change the purpose of use of personal information only when it is reasonably recognized that the purpose after the change is related to the purpose before the change.

2. When the purpose of use is changed, the Company will notify users of the changed purpose by the method determined by the Company or publish it on the App.

5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

1. The Company will not provide personal information to third parties without the prior consent of users, except in the following cases. However, this excludes cases permitted by the Personal Information Protection Act and other laws.

  • When necessary to protect human life, body, or property and it is difficult to obtain the consent of the person
  • When particularly necessary to improve public health or promote the sound development of children and it is difficult to obtain the consent of the person
  • When it is necessary to cooperate with national institutions, local governments, or their consignees in carrying out affairs stipulated by laws and obtaining consent may hinder the execution of such affairs
  • When the following matters are announced or published in advance and the Company has reported to the Personal Information Protection Commission

2. Notwithstanding the provisions of the preceding paragraph, the following cases shall not be considered as provision to third parties:

  • When the Company entrusts all or part of the handling of personal information within the scope necessary to achieve the purpose of use
  • When personal information is provided due to business succession through merger or other reasons
  • When personal information is jointly used with specific parties and the person has been notified in advance of the fact, the items of personal information jointly used, the scope of joint users, the purpose of use by users, and the name or title of the person responsible for managing the personal information, or the person can easily know it

6. Disclosure of Personal Information

1. When the Company is requested to disclose personal information by the person, it will disclose it to the person without delay. However, if disclosure would fall under any of the following cases, the Company may not disclose all or part of it, and if it decides not to disclose, it will notify the person without delay:

  • When there is a risk of harming the life, body, property, or other rights and interests of the person or a third party
  • When there is a risk of significantly hindering the proper implementation of the Company's business
  • When it would violate other laws

2. Notwithstanding the provisions of the preceding paragraph, information other than personal information such as history information and characteristic information will not be disclosed in principle.

7. Correction and Deletion of Personal Information

1. Users may request the Company to correct, add, or delete personal information (hereinafter "correction, etc.") through the procedures determined by the Company when the personal information held by the Company is incorrect.

2. When the Company receives a request from users under the preceding paragraph and determines that the request is reasonable, it will correct the personal information without delay.

3. The Company will notify users without delay when it has made corrections based on the provisions of the preceding paragraph or when it has decided not to make corrections.

8. Suspension of Use of Personal Information

1. When the Company is requested by the person to suspend or delete the use of personal information (hereinafter "suspension of use, etc.") on the grounds that the personal information is handled beyond the scope of the purpose of use or that it was obtained by fraudulent means, the Company will conduct necessary investigations without delay.

2. Based on the investigation results of the preceding paragraph, when it is determined that the request is reasonable, the Company will suspend the use of the personal information without delay.

3. The Company will notify the person without delay when it has suspended use based on the provisions of the preceding paragraph or when it has decided not to suspend use.

4. Notwithstanding the preceding two paragraphs, when suspension of use would require excessive costs or when it is difficult to suspend use and alternative measures that can protect the user's rights can be taken, such alternative measures will be taken.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

1. The contents of this Policy may be changed without notifying users, except for matters stipulated by laws and other matters stipulated in this Policy.

2. The changed Privacy Policy will take effect by posting it on the App or notifying users.

10. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding this Policy, please contact the following:

  • Email: privacy@mathstep.app
  • General inquiries: support@mathstep.app

11. Use of Cookies

1. This App may use cookies to improve user convenience.

2. Cookies are small text files that websites send to users' computers. Cookies can record users' usage status and setting information.

3. Users can refuse to receive cookies by setting their browser, but some functions of this App may not be available in that case.

12. Access Analysis Tools

The Company may use the following access analysis tools to understand the usage status of this App:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase Analytics

These tools collect access information in a form that cannot identify individuals and use it to improve services.

13. Data Retention Period

The Company will retain collected personal information for the following periods:

  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Usage history: For the period specified by law
  • Inquiry information: For 1 year after response completion
