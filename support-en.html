Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I delete my math expression history?

A: You can delete your history at any time within the app. Go to Settings > History > Clear All Data.

Q: Is my data stored on your servers?

A: No, all math expressions are stored locally on your device. Only the expressions you input are sent to OpenAI API for analysis.

Q: Can children under 13 use this app safely?

A: Yes, our app is designed to be safe for children. We don't collect personal information from users of any age.

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: Please email us at info@profilecode.codes with details about the issue, including your device model and app version.

Q: Will you add new features?

A: Yes! We're constantly working to improve the app. Follow our updates through the app store.
